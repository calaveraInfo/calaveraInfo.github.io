My life

Since I'm looking for a job right now, it's wise to have this portfolio/resume page. So, who am I?

My name is František Řezáč, I was born in 1983 in Czech Republic and I'm currently living in Prague. I was interested in computers since childhood (apart from being an aircraft pilot, but that stopped with RC models). My first Linux was Red Hat 5.2 (anyone remembers pre-enterprise Red hat era?) during high school studies. However I hated math and all that exact science. Mainly because my father, Ivan, is an actor (a good one) and I grew up in a very "liberal" environment.

It changed when I was forced to pass final examination in Mathematics and Physics. I changed my mind and I went to University of West Bohemia and graduated there with Master degree in Distributed Systems. I also successfully passed all 4 semesters of CCNA.

With my grandfather being a chief of Institute of Modern Languages I learned English soon and traveled a lot during my whole life. USA, Europe, India. You name it. I love nature and especialy mountains and that led me to hitchiking, mountaineering and mountain climbing. I also joined my good friend, Adam Wolf, who is leading a group of children dedicated to woodcraft idea.

I was also working during my studies. I tried PHP company, Java company, I was a freelancer. Apart from what is writen in my CV, my recent non-commercial work can be seen at following places:

  • Web pages of my personal friend, cloathing designer Iva Šimandlová. I created PHP backend (from scratch) and coded graphic design into HTML. One of my first real works in web application industry.
  • Web pages of Vyšehrátky theatre festival. I created the content management system on top of SilverStripe CMS.
  • Web pages of my mountaineering club. This is a Drupal based web and I'm responsible for everything there.
  • My current "obsession" is Weblib PHP framework. Since I released it under Apache license, everyone can review my coding skills there.

It's hard to describe my life in a few paragraphs:-).
